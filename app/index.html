Riding lessons

Riding lessons for beginners

Class levels provide a sequential order of riding classes based on our systematic approach to teaching riding. As our students achieve the skills required for each level, they progress from one class to the next in their chosen discipline.

Horseback riding

This training course is only possible after successful completion of a primary school course. Rider needs special recommendation from the Head coach of the School. The duration of the basic course is 1 year, under condition of training at least 3 times a week. The recommended number of lessons is 3 - 4 times a week.

Riding lessons for advanced dressage / showjumping

The next stage after the basic skills of riding - professional training. There are several main areas of equestrian sport: competition and departure. Our trainings - athletes, constantly improve their qualifications in the arenas of national and international competitions, will help you to gain new skills and become a true athlete.

Photosession

Want to get amazing portraits and photos with our horses, on a black background or in the nature, on horseback or near a horse? You can come with your photographer and take the cinema you like to rent for a photo session, or we can invite one of our photographers for you.
